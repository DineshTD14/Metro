Public Notice

It has come to our notice that some unscrupulous elements are trying to mislead the candidates / public by spreading false details / information regarding the job opportunities in CMRL in the social media and local TV channels. Hence, it is brought to the notice of the candidates / public that the recruitment advertisements of CMRL are being published time through open advertisement only in newspaper and CMRL website “https://chennaimetrorail.org”. Therefore, candidates / public are hereby requested to go through our website regarding employment opportunities in CMRL and not to rely and act only based on the misleading information being published in the social media and local TV channels. Further, CMRL is not responsible for any losses incurred by the individual on account of the misleading false details / information’s disseminated in the social media and local TV channels.
